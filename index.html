FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about our custom printing and engraving services? Find answers here.

What file formats do you accept for design uploads?

We accept high-resolution files in PDF, AI (Adobe Illustrator), PNG, JPG, and JPEG formats. For laser engraving and MDF/acrylic cutting, vector files (.AI, .PDF, or .SVG) are highly recommended to ensure precise cutting paths and optimal print quality.

What is the turnaround time for custom orders?

Our standard turnaround time is 2 to 4 business days depending on the project volume and service type. For urgent needs, same-day or next-day express printing options are available for local pickup at our Dhobi Talao units in Mumbai.

Do you have a minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

No, we do not enforce any strict minimum order quantities. We cater to single custom personal gifts, small batch prototype runs, as well as high-volume roll-based orders for businesses. No job is too small or too large for us.

What materials can be laser engraved or UV printed?

Our flatbed UV printer can directly print on wood, glass, metals, acrylics, leather, and plastic substrates. Laser engraving is perfect for metal markings, wooden items, personalized corporate gifts, and acrylic signage. Precision MDF cutting is suitable for wood, MDF, acrylic, and composite boards.

What are UV DTF stickers and how do they work?

UV DTF (Direct to Film) stickers are high-quality transfers printed with a raised gloss/varnish finish. You simply peel the transfer film, apply it onto any hard, clean surface (like mugs, water bottles, laptops, glass, or plastic), rub it firmly, and peel the clear top film off. The sticker transfers perfectly, looking like a direct print.

Do you ship nationwide across India?

Yes! We ship our customized print products, UV DTF sticker rolls, and engraved items to all major cities and states across India. We also support self-pickup from our physical shop units in Mumbai for customers who need their orders immediately.
